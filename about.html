About Me

An overview of my extraordinary, ordinary life.

I am currently sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania studying Computer Science and Business in the Jerome Fisher Program in Management and Technology. I am interested in startups and coming up with new ideas. During the school year, I also work as a teaching assistant for CIS 240: Introduction to Computer Architecture and Business Develpoment Intern at Tesorio.

I grew up in Minnesota with my mother, father, and older sister. I've spent the majority of my life in Minnetonka, MN, where I played with amazing neighbors, learned from extraordinary teachers, found that there will always be people there to pick me up when I fall. Even though I'm on the East Coast now, I will never forget where I come from.

My favorite book is The Giving Tree by Shel Silverstein. My favorite movie is Gattaca. My favorite TV show is Game of Thrones. My favorite food is my mother's dumplings. My favorite color is blue. My favorite holiday is Thanksgiving Day. My favorite super power is magnetism, like Magneto. My favorite board game is Settlers of Catan. And my favorite animal is the polar bear.
